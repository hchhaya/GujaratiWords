Confusing Gujarati words

These are similar-sounding words that sometimes confuse new learners of Gujarati.

  • મળવાનુ - maLvaanu - to meet
  • મરવાનુ - marvaanu - to die
  • ગાવાનુ - gaavaanu - to sing
  • ગાળવાનુ - gaaLvanu - to filter
  • ગોર - gor - priest
  • ગોળ - goL - circle OR jaggery
  • ડોલ - dol - bucket
  • ઢોલ - dhol - drum
  • પાદવાનુ - padvaanu - to fart
  • પાળવાનુ - Paalvaanu - to tame
  • પાડવાનુ - paaDVaanu - to make fall
  • ગોરા - goraa - fair
  • ગોળા - goLaa - spheres
  • ગોધા - godhaa - oxen
  • ગોઘા - goghaa - confused
  • ધોડા - ghoDaa - horses
  • બળવાનુ - baLvaanu - to burn
  • ભરવાનુ - bharvaanu - to fill
  • ભણવાનુ - bhaNvaanu - to study
  • ભળવાનુ - bhaLvaanu - to mix
  • છાંટવાનુ - chhaatvanu - to spray
  • ચાટવાનુ - chaatvanu - to lick
  • ફાંસ - phaans - splinter
  • ફણસ - phaNas - jackfruit
  • ફાનસ - phaanas - lantern
  • જાડા - jaaDaa - fat
  • ઝાડા - jhaaDaa - diarrhea
  • જાળા - jaaLaa - spider web
  • તળવાનુ - taLvaanu - to fry
  • દળવાનુ - daLvaanu - to grind
  • ઢળવાનુ - dhaLvaanu - to lean
